A Few Words About Our Farm

Farm is made up of two certified organic properties,
where our farmers are constantly growing organic
vegetables, crops, and fruits of the highest quality in the US.

King Uncle offers the widest range of raw and flavored products
and has in-house dedicated processing facilities for Almonds,
Cashews, Walnuts, Pistachios, Dates, Seeds, Raisins, and Berries.
All products are packed and stored in a temperature-controlled facility,
and the company integrates new technologies to achieve high hygiene
standards required for the ever-growing segment.

King Uncle Dry Fruits

King Uncle, originally incorporated in 2002 as
Krishna Enterprises, is a professionally managed organization with over
5 decades of experience in the field of spices and dry fruits.
In 2014, the company acquired its current name and began importing
and domestically purchasing premium quality products from around the world.

As one of the largest importers of dates, raisins, pistachios,
dry figs, and many other products, King Uncle travels the
world to procure the very best nuts and dry fruits, each region has to offer.
The company is committed to providing world-class services
along with quality products to its clients, and aspires to achieve
long-term relationships based on strong corporate ethics and unmatched quality.

Storing Your Favourite Dates, Dry Fruits and Nuts!

For immediate usage, store these products in airtight containers at cool, dark spot reducing the exposure of light, oxygen and moisture.

Do not expose to heat as it may go rancid or create grounds for soft bodied animals.

For longer user, keep it refrigerated giving it the right taste and more flavourful vibrant to these tasteful Products.
